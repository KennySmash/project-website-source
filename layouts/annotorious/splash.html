Integrate anywhere. Annotorious has no server-side dependencies and makes no assumptions about your technology stack or backend. Roll your own custom integration with the JavaScript API, or follow one of the guides for simple out-of-the-box recipes.

Customize & extend. Create your own extension components for the annotation editor. Change the look and feel of everything with CSS. Use formatters to apply rule-based styles to annotations.

Annotorious is also available as a plugin to OpenSeadragon, a JavaScript viewer for high-resolution zoomable images. Learn more.
